By: Dreylon Nguyen

Timeline

  • 2012: Icona Pop releases “I Love It (feat. Charli XCX)”
  • 2014: Charli XCX releases “Fancy feat. Iggy Azalea” and “Boom Clap”
  • 2017: Lorde’s album “Melodrama” nominated for Album of the Year in 2018
  • 2024: Charli XCX releases “brat,” featuring the song “Girl, so confusing”

Why are they being compared?

In a 2014 interview, Charli XCX was asked about the inspiration behind “Royals”. Many compare Charli and Lorde due to their similar musical styles and curly hair.
