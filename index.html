The History of "Girl, so confusing"

By: Dreylon Nguyen

Timeline

  • 2012: Icona Pop releases “I Love It (feat. Charli XCX)”
  • 2014: Charli XCX releases “Fancy feat. Iggy Azalea” and “Boom Clap”
  • 2013: Lorde releases her debut album "Pure Heroine"
  • 2017: Lorde’s album “Melodrama” nominated for Album of the Year in 2018
  • 2024: Charli XCX releases “brat,” featuring the song “Girl, so confusing”

The Song

★ Tenth track off of Charli XCX’s sixth studio album “brat” released on June 7, 2024

★ About being a girl in the modern age

★ Discusses an unknown female musician with whom Charli has a strained relationship

Why are they being compared?

In a 2014 interview, Charli XCX was asked about the inspiration behind “Royals”. Many compare Charli and Lorde due to their similar musical styles and curly hair.

Charli XCX = curly hair + singer | Lorde = curly hair + singer

Does Charli XCX equal Lorde?

Charli was constantly comparing herself to the mainstream success that Lorde had reached.

"Girl, so confusing" feat. Lorde

On June 24, 2024, Charli XCX released a remix featuring Lorde.
